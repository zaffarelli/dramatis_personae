Lifepaths

All characters are build in dP around an historical approach: each step gives the characters a certain number of attributes, skills and other points. A few of them are custom steps that allow customization.

The standard construction of a character is:

  • One Birthright matching to her species (140pts)
  • Upbringing (20pts or in two parts with 15 + 5 pts)
  • Apprenticeship (25 pts)
  • Early Career (48 pts)
  • Some Tours of duty (20 pts each, a begining PC has two of them)
  • Worldly Benefits (7 pts)
